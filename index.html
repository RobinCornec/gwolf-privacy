Privacy Policy – GWolf

This privacy policy explains how the GWolf mobile application handles your data.

1. Data Collection

GWolf does not collect or store any personal data. All scores and game history are stored locally on your device.

2. Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection and does not send any information to external servers.

3. Analytics

GWolf does not use any analytics services or tracking tools.

4. Third-party Services

The app does not use third-party SDKs or services that access your data.

5. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this policy, feel free to contact us at robincornec@gmail.com.
